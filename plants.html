The Swiss Cheese plant likes bright indirect light and to be watered around once every 2 weeks.

Pothos likes bright indirect light and to be watered a little less than once a week.

Monsteras get big and prefer bright indirect light and to be watered around once a week.

Alocasias like medium to bright indirect light and to be watered once a week, sometimes more.

Philodendrons like medium to bright indirect light and to be watered around once every 2 weeks.

Succulents like bright light and want to be watered around once a month.
