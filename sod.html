Sod

Our SOD is one of our most prideful items that we carry. The results and feedback from customers (and landscapers/contractors) have been 100% positive with our current North New Jersey grower. We know we have something special.

We know SOD is a long-term investment that homeowners make, that is why we bring in SOD that is cut thick with an established root system. This allows the SOD to stay hydrated longer and allows the roots to continue to grow right into your existing soil. Not only do the roots grow quick, but because there are so many roots intact, the color quality of the grass stays vibrant as it grows.

Not only do we carry the best SOD you will find, but we always will explain to you in-depth how to take care of your new lawn. We will show you the correct steps to take to get your new lawn established and how to keep it flourishing throughout the rest of the year.
